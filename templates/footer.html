About Us

Greetings and welcome to our online simulator platform developed by a group of passionate students from the Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) Dharwad. Our mission is to provide an interactive and educational experience that transcends the traditional boundaries of learning. Whether you are a student looking to supplement your classroom learning, an educator searching for innovative teaching tools, or a curious individual eager to explore new subjects, our online simulator platform welcomes you with open arms. Join us in the exciting journey of discovery, exploration, and learning.
